Transition Care

Recovering from a surgery, critical illness, or unexpected health setback is a challenging journey. While hospitals focus on emergency treatment and discharge, the immediate needs of patients following their hospital stay require careful consideration. Patients are still struggling with the traumas, both physically and psychologically, even after being deemed stable and ready for discharge.

Transition Care

  • Avoid hospital-acquired infections
    Preventing patients and health workers from being harmed by avoidable infections.
  • Fast Recovery
    Specialized care to ease patient recovery after hospital stay. Safe process and 24/7 medical team observation and availability. Peace of mind during recovery process.
  • Perfect Diet Plan
    Perfect diet plan enriched with the right nutrients and elements.
  • Medical Supervision and Health Support
    Patient receives significant amount of medical supervision and health support from our Nursing and Physio staff that they need to gain the physical and mental strength.
  • Reduce Hospital Readmission or Visits
    Ongoing support and care co-ordination to reduce the hospital readmission or visits. Improve overall health and well-being and promote independence.

Experience exceptional post-hospitalization care at HealthOnUs center. Our specialized nursing specialists and therapists provide personalized transitional care plans, accelerating your recovery and minimizing hospital readmissions.
