    Physiotherapy: Improve your physical fitness

    Physiotherapy, also known as physical therapy, is a specialized healthcare profession to enhance and restore physical functions and mobility of a person suffering from illness, injury, or disability. It involves a comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, and personalized care plan to improve pain, physical strength, and physical movement
