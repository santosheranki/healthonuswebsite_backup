FAQs

Pharmacists dispense medications and provide expertise in their safe use to promote healthier lives. They also conduct health screenings, administer immunizations, and oversee medication adherence.

Pharmacists are responsible for verifying and dispensing medications, compounding pharmacy medicines if necessary, and providing health advice based on symptom analysis.

A good pharmacist possesses excellent moral character, honesty in dealing with patients regarding their medications, and takes responsibility for patient care through effective communication and meeting individual needs while maintaining professionalism.

Pharmacists work closely with Pharmacy Technicians to provide patient education, recommend appropriate medications, and prepare medications if required.
