Our Journey

The timeline encapsulates our journey, reflecting our growth, achievements, and relentless pursuit of providing top-notch services to our clients.

  • Jan 2022
    The inception of the HealthOnUs app, a brainchild of healthcare and tech maestros, marked the beginning of our journey.
  • Jul 2023
    The soft launch of the HealthOnUs app took place, introducing our homecare services to communities.
  • Aug 2023
    We hit the ground running with the full-fledged commencement of our Homecare services in the cities of Hyderabad and Visakhapatnam.
  • Sep 2023
    A significant milestone was achieved with the successful completion of 100 services, a testament to our commitment and dedication.
  • Oct 2023
    The expansion of our footprint continued with the inauguration of new centers in Hyderabad.
  • Nov 2023
    Furthering our reach, we inaugurated new centers in Visakhapatnam, bringing our services closer to the people
  • Dec 2023
    The year ended on a high note with establishment of the operation team.
  • Feb 2024
    Grand launch of the HealthOnUs App by Shri Pawan Kalyan
Mission

To deliver a superior healthcare services experience beyond traditional hospital settings to residences and communities. By consolidating all healthcare services under one roof, we strive to assist individuals on their health journey with an abundance of love and the care they deserve.

Vision

To create a world where people won’t have to compromise on the quality of care they need. We want to emerge as a robust healthcare provider that caters to everyone’s needs for a healthy and happy life, powered by cutting-edge technology, and steered by a strong set of values.
